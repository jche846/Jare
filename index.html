Our Services

Environmental Consulting

Aquagate specialises in the environmental sector, whether it be ecology, water quality, natural resources or contaminated land we aim to offer the best solutions. We have a strong passion for sustainable design and our mission is to keep New Zealand one of the greenest countries in the world.

Engineering Design

Our experienced engineers in the design team makes it sure that you are dealing with the best. Aquate gate have been successfully involved in complex projects including large dams, reservoirs , wind farms and hydro generation plants. No project is too large for our liking and we always have a ‘can-do’ attitude towards an optimal design.

Project Management

A lot of our success for complex projects can be contributed to our strong focus on project management. Aquagate has the personnel, software, and experience to make sure that your project is completed on time, on budget and to a high standard.
